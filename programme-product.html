FAQ

Common Questions

Is this for product managers, engineers, or both?+

Both. The sprint is designed so PMs and engineers can take it together and build shared workflows — or independently, with tracks tailored to each role. Week 3 has separate applied tracks for PM and engineering disciplines.

We already use AI tools. Will this still add value?+

Yes. The gap is rarely tool access — it is systematic workflow integration. The sprint takes teams from ad-hoc AI use to consistent, measurable capability embedded in how you actually ship.

Is this relevant for non-technical product managers?+

Yes. No coding experience is required. Non-technical PMs focus on discovery, strategy, and AI-assisted specification workflows. The engineering track is available but not mandatory.

Can we run this as a team?+

Yes — and we recommend it. Teams that go through the sprint together build shared workflows and shared vocabulary. Corporate seat pricing is available for 2+ participants.
