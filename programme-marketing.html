FAQ

Common Questions

Will AI replace copywriters and creative teams?+

Not the good ones. This is addressed directly in Week 1. AI removes the volume burden — research, first drafts, formatting — freeing your creative professionals for the high-judgment work AI cannot replicate.

Is this relevant for B2B or B2C marketing?+

Both. The core curriculum covers principles that apply across contexts. Week 3 projects can be tailored to B2B pipeline work, B2C campaigns, or brand management depending on your role.

How do I ensure AI content is on-brand?+

Brand governance with AI is covered explicitly in Week 2. You will build a brand-safe AI content framework — including tone of voice guidance, fact-checking workflows, and approval gates.

Can I bring live campaigns into the sprint?+

Yes. The capstone is designed around a real marketing or sales challenge you are currently facing. You will leave with a deployed workflow, not just a theory.
