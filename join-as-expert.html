The Process

How the Expert
Partnership Works

1

Apply & Verify

Submit your application. We review your professional background, applied AI experience, and domain track record. For shortlisted applicants, we schedule a brief 20-minute verification call — not an interview, a professional conversation.

2

Onboarding & Orientation

Accepted experts complete a short Brixgate orientation — delivery standards, cohort management tools, assessment criteria, and the Expert Practitioner Code of Conduct. Typically 2–3 hours total.

3

Matched to Your First Cohort

Once verified and onboarded, we match you to a cohort in your field as soon as one is ready. Brixgate handles all participant recruitment, scheduling, and logistics ahead of your first session.

4

Deliver. Assess. Get Paid.

You deliver the 4-week sprint. Issue practitioner assessments. Revenue share is transferred to you within 5 business days of cohort close. No admin — just delivery and payment.

Qualification Criteria

Who We Are Looking For

We set a high bar — because the professionals we serve deserve only the most credible practitioners.

✓

5+ Years of Active Professional Practice in Your Field

You are mid-senior to executive level. You are not at the start of your career. You have built a professional track record that commands credibility with other experienced practitioners.

✓

Verified, Applied AI Experience in Your Specific Domain

You have actually deployed AI in your profession — not just used consumer tools. You have built workflows, integrated tools, or led AI initiatives in real professional contexts. You can name what you did, what tools you used, and what it produced.

✓

Demonstrable, Measurable Results

You can point to specific, quantified outcomes from your AI application. Productivity gains, cost reductions, quality improvements, risk mitigation — not "I experimented with it" but "we did X and it produced Y."

✓

Communication and Teaching Ability

Domain expertise alone is not enough. Brixgate Expert Practitioners must be able to make complex applied knowledge learnable and actionable for other professionals. We assess this directly in your application.

✓

Commitment to High-Quality Delivery

A Brixgate cohort is a commitment. We expect Expert Practitioners to arrive prepared, engaged, and invested in participant outcomes. Participant assessments and cohort ratings are published on your profile.

✓

Values Alignment: Ethics, Accountability, Integrity

You operate to the highest professional and ethical standards. You model the Character pillar — deploying AI responsibly and transparently, and serving practitioners in their genuine professional interest, not just completing a delivery contract.

✓

Professional English and Remote Delivery Capability

Brixgate cohorts are delivered live online to professionals across Africa, the UK, and internationally. Expert Practitioners must be fluent in professional English and comfortable leading engaging, structured sessions via video with 10–25 participants.

A Note on Who We're Looking For

We set a high bar deliberately. The professionals who pay to learn from you have real jobs, real stakes, and real peers they answer to. They deserve only practitioners who have genuinely done what they're being taught. We review every application personally — and we decline more than we accept. That is what makes the Expert Practitioner designation worth having.
