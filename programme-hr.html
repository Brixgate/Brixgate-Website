FAQ

Common Questions

Is this programme suitable for small HR teams?+

Yes. Whether you are a team of 2 or 200, the sprint is built around your specific HR challenges. Cohort-based delivery means you learn from peers at different scales.

Will AI make better hiring decisions?+

Not on its own — and we are direct about this. The programme teaches you how to use AI to surface better information faster, while keeping human judgment at the centre of consequential people decisions.

Does the programme cover GDPR and data protection for HR AI?+

Yes. Data governance, employee consent, and regulatory compliance are covered in Week 1 and embedded throughout. You will leave with a framework for ethical AI deployment.

Can I bring my organisation HR challenges into the sprint?+

Yes — and we encourage it. The capstone is designed around a real challenge from your own people function.
