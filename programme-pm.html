FAQs

Common Questions

Is this for Agile or Waterfall project managers? +
Both. The AI workflow principles apply across all delivery methodologies. The sprint covers Agile-native AI tools alongside traditional PM platforms. Your capstone is built around your actual methodology.
Does this help with PMI or PRINCE2 certification prep? +
This sprint does not replace certification prep. It complements your existing methodology by showing you how to apply AI within the governance frameworks you already use.
What if I manage projects without a formal PMO? +
The sprint is equally valuable for standalone PMs. The AI tools and workflows covered are applicable at the individual project level — you do not need enterprise PMO infrastructure to benefit.
Can I apply this to an active project during the sprint? +
Yes — and we encourage it. The capstone is designed around a real project challenge you are currently managing.
