FAQ

Common Questions

Is this safe for use in actual clinical practice?+

Yes — with the right governance. The programme is built around responsible AI adoption. You will learn how to apply AI and how to ensure it is clinically safe, auditable, and compliant with patient data regulations.

Do I need a technical background?+

No. The programme is designed for clinicians and healthcare administrators, not developers. You will work with tools already approved for healthcare environments.

What if my hospital IT policy restricts cloud AI tools?+

We cover locally-deployable and on-premise options for restricted environments. Your capstone can be designed around the constraints of your specific institution.

Is patient data used during the sprint?+

No patient data is used or shared. All exercises use synthetic or anonymised datasets. Your institution data governance is not implicated by participation.
