The Sprint

4 Weeks. Full Transformation.

Intensive by design. Every week builds on the last. You leave week four with a deployed AI workflow, applied deliverables, and certification — not a certificate of attendance.

Week 1
Foundation & Mapping
Map the AI landscape in your field. Audit your current tools. Identify the highest-leverage opportunities in your specific role. Scope your first applied project.

The Framework

Three Pillars.
One Transformation.

Every Brixgate programme is built around three inseparable pillars. Develop all three and you don't just know how to use AI — you become the kind of professional who leads with it.

01
Capacity
Technical and conceptual fluency to use AI effectively in your specific field. Not general AI literacy — deep, field-specific knowledge of which tools to use, when, and how to interpret their outputs in your professional context.
02
Character
Professional judgment to deploy AI ethically, accountably, and confidently. In every field, AI creates decision points where you must exercise judgment and take accountability. Character is what separates an Expert Practitioner from someone who just runs prompts.
03
Cadence
Habits and workflows that embed AI into daily practice so it compounds over time. The professionals who gain the most from AI are those who have made it habitual — not a tool they reach for occasionally, but a rhythm that delivers returns every day.

Where Are You on the

How would you describe your current use of AI tools in your day-to-day professional work?
