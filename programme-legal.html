FAQ

Common Questions

Is AI-generated legal analysis reliable enough to use professionally?+

With the right verification framework — yes, for many tasks. The programme teaches you exactly where AI adds reliable value (research, first drafts, summarisation) and where human judgment must remain paramount.

What about client confidentiality when using AI tools?+

This is addressed directly in Week 1. You will learn how to evaluate tool data retention policies, configure enterprise tools safely, and establish an AI tool selection framework that protects privilege.

Is this relevant to Nigerian law practice?+

Yes. While the tools are global, the regulatory framing covers NBA rules, Nigerian data protection requirements, and specific challenges of AI adoption in the Nigerian legal market.

Can paralegals and legal ops professionals join?+

Absolutely. The programme is designed for anyone with a professional legal function — not just qualified lawyers. Legal operations, compliance, and regulatory affairs professionals are all well-served.
