FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need data science skills?+

No. The programme is designed for operations professionals, not data scientists. You will use tools that surface intelligence from your data without requiring you to write algorithms from scratch.

What if our supply chain data is messy or incomplete?+

Most real supply chains have imperfect data. Week 1 includes working with imperfect datasets. You will leave with approaches that work in the real world, not just in clean test environments.

Is this relevant to African supply chain challenges specifically?+

Yes. Logistics infrastructure, cross-border trade complexity, and currency volatility are threaded through the applied examples. Your practitioner has built these systems in the African context.

Can I apply the sprint to a current supply chain problem?+

Yes. The capstone is designed around a challenge from your own operation. You will leave with a deployed solution, not a simulation.
